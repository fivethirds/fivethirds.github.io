How to use:
The bottom left text indicates the current mode: Add components, delete components, or analyze. Press M to change mode.
In 'add components' mode, click any empty space to create a new node. To create a parent-child relationship between two nodes, click the parent first, then the child. Click and drag any node to rearrange.
In 'delete components' mode, click any node to delete it.
In 'analyze' mode, double click any node to analyze its genetics. To compare the genetics of two nodes, click both.
